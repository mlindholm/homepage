About

I'm a product designer from Helsinki, currently working at Neverthink.

I like to think of my self as a generalist with a keen eye for aesthetics and usability. I'm comfortable in working with a wide range of abstraction levels, from clarifying the concept to tweaking the code. I've worked with clients in business sectors like aviation, travel, telecommunications, insurance and entertainment.

You can find me on Twitter, Dribble, Flickr and Github.
