About Mathias

I’m a designer from Helsinki with a love for mountain biking, pizza, craft beer and the great outdoors.

For the past 10 years I’ve worked as a product designer making successful apps, tools and whatnot in outstanding teams. I’ve worked in business sectors like aviation, telecommunications, insurance and entertainment. Currently I’m working at Neverthink as VP of Product.

I’m a maker of things, a curious tinkerer, and sometimes I bite more than I can chew – though I think it’s a good trait :-)
